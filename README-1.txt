  □ Study in groups - discuss concepts with classmates
  □ Use active recall - test yourself, don't just re-read
  □ Spaced repetition - review concepts over multiple days
  □ Practice problems - at least 70% of study time
  □ Take breaks - 5 min per 25 min study (Pomodoro technique)
  □ Eliminate distractions - phone off, quiet space
  □ Get enough sleep - sleep is when memory consolidates
  □ Exercise - physical activity improves focus and memory
